Purchasing Details

The pricing of each service is dependent on the type of art or photos the clients request and the time taken for each service:

  • Digital photos - Our digital photographers are well equipped to provide you with the best quality images

    • Whether you want portfolios, y2k themed, cinematic themed or nature themed photographs you can schedule an appointment with us or visit the gallery in person and discuss with our highly talented photographers and have your photos taken.

  • Wall Art - Our artists and painters are always on the clock ready to ensure they capture your interests and bring them to life through art.

    • To purchase any art, the client can choose to either attend our gallery events in person or in the case where they need to purchase art immediately, visit our website for regular updates on available art and prchase online. Delivery takes about a week or more depending on the customer's location.

    We have also recently introduced appointment sessions for our clients whereby they get to describe their ideas/visions to our artists and have their own art curated from scratch.
